WHO ARE WE ?

Loyola Ambulance and Funeral Arrangers, serving the community since 2003, we a trusted provider of comprehensive funeral and ambulance services. Our company offers 24/7 ambulance and hearse van services, we also undertake teak and decorative coffins, mobile mortuary/iceboxes, and designable grave monuments. They also specialize in granite stone letter carvings and operate a well-equipped mortuary facility in a reputed hospital in Moodbidri, ensuring dignified and respectful arrangements for every family they serve.

Our Services Offer

Teak, Veneer & Decorative Coffins

We offer a thoughtfully selected range of coffins to suit every need, each crafted with care, respect, and attention to detail. Our teak coffins are made from solid hardwood, providing timeless beauty, durability, and a rich natural finish, adding premium quality to the last rites. For a more affordable yet still premium option, our veneer coffins feature high-quality wood finishes over a sturdy core, blending appearance and value. We also offer decorative coffins, categorized into Special Class, First Class, Second Class, and Third Class.

Our Ambulance

We offer a range of specialized ambulances to meet different needs. Our fleet includes two oxygen-supported ambulances for patients requiring respiratory assistance, a dedicated mortuary ambulance for the dignified transport of deceased individuals, and a hearse van for ceremonial purposes. Each vehicle is well-equipped, clean, and maintained to ensure safety, hygiene, and respect in every situation. Whether it's an emergency or a final journey, we provide reliable and professional service around the clock.

Mortuary Services

We offer mobile mortuary services that can be delivered to your doorstep, providing a convenient and respectful option for families. Our VIP mortuary units come with transport facilities and are equipped with viewing lights, allowing you to see your loved ones clearly during the final moments. Additionally, we have a mortuary facility available at Alvas Hospital, ensuring professional care and proper preservation at all times.

Grave Monuments, Carvings and Floral Design

We specialize in crafting high-quality grave monuments using premium, water-resistant granite, ensuring both durability and elegance. Our services include expert letter carvings in granite, personalized to reflect your chosen designs and preferences. We also offer a wide variety of floral arrangements, thoughtfully designed to honor your loved ones with beauty and respect. Your selected styles and ideas are given the highest priority, making each monument a unique and meaningful tribute.
